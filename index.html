Strawberry Milkshake

Ingrident list:

  • Ice cream
  • cream
  • milk
  • vanilla
  • Strawberrys

Directions

  1. Freeze the glasses that you’ll serve the shakes in so that they stay cold and don’t melt too quickly.
  2. Allow the ice cream to soften slightly. You don’t want it runny, but allowing it to become just a little soft will help it blend easily.
  3. Add the ice cream, milk, cream and vanilla to the mixer. Add in any fruit (strawberries!) or other mix-ins such as chocolate or caramel.
  4. Blend until smooth! We use a Ninja blender because they are strong, big and powerful
